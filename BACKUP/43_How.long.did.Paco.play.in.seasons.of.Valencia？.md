<p>Valencia Seasons:</p>
<ul>
<li>2013-14 Season(56games * 90minutes)： Paco played 1478 /5040minutes = 29.3%</li>
<li>2014-15 Season(42games * 90minutes)： Paco played 2402 /3780minutes = 63.5%</li>
<li>2015-16 Season(58 games * 90minutes)： Paco played 3177 /5220minutes = 60.8%</li>
<li>2016-17 Season is similar to 2014-15 season, Paco may play about 2500 minutes.</li>
</ul>
<p><img src="https://pacoxu.wordpress.com/wp-content/uploads/2016/09/e5b18fe5b995e688aae59bbe-2016-09-03-13-35-07.png" alt="屏幕截图 2016-09-03 13.35.07.png" /></p>
<p>This season started and Valencia has already played 2 matches. Paco played 90 minutes for Valencia, while Barcelona played 4 games including 2 games of Spanish Super Cup with Sevilla.</p>
<p>For prediction, Barcelona may head to Top 4 in Copa del Rey and Championship, they may play about 60 games. In following 56 games, most fans thinks he can play 30%-40%   of  5040minutes, which would be about 1500 minutes to 2000 minutes.</p>
<p>As Munir got more than 15000 minutes last season, we may be optimism to current situation of Paco.</p>
<p>In past 3 seasons, Paco scored 14 goals in 13-14 &amp; 14-15 and 15 goals in 15-16. So, 15 goals may be goal of 15-16 season in Barcelona.</p>
